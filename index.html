Welcome

My name is Nawaraj Dahal. But if you're connected to the Department of Mathematics and Statistics at SJSU or went to De Anza College during my time there (Sept. 2022 - Jun. 2025), then there is a non-zero chance that you know me simply as "Nawa".

I'm an undergraduate student in Applied and Computational Mathematics at SJSU. My research interests lie in nonlinear partial differential equations, numerical analysis, and scientific computing, particularly in problems where physical modeling, mathematical analysis, and computation interact. I am also interested in the analysis of numerical methods used to solve PDEs. I am fascinated by the questions of convergence, stability, conditioning, and approximation, as well as their applications to PDE models arising in physics and other applied sciences.

This website serves as a place to share my mathematical notes, research reflections, and my academic journey as I continue exploring mathematics. Basically a place to nerd out about the mathematical questions that keep me up at night.
